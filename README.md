# 🌱 VayuSense - AI Powered Urban Air Quality Intelligence Platform

## Overview

VayuSense is an AI-powered air quality intelligence platform designed to help users monitor, analyze, and understand urban air pollution through an interactive and intelligent dashboard.

The platform combines environmental data visualization, air quality analysis, forecasting, pollution insights, and AI assistance to transform complex pollution data into meaningful information.

The goal of VayuSense is to support smarter environmental decisions and create awareness about air quality conditions.

---

## Problem Statement

Air pollution is a growing challenge in urban areas. Although air quality data is available through different sources, it is often difficult to interpret and use effectively.

People and organizations need a platform that can:

- Monitor air quality conditions
- Understand pollution trends
- Compare air quality between locations
- Identify possible pollution sources
- Generate useful environmental insights

VayuSense provides a unified solution for these challenges.
